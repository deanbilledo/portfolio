Day 2 - June 9 ‚Ä¢ Corporate Immersion Begins

Early start to Pasig City for our first corporate visit at Foundever Philippines (formerly Sitel Group). This global CX leader serving 45+ languages showed us how AI, cloud computing, and data analytics transform customer service. Interactive sessions with their operations and IT teams revealed insights into customer journey mapping, digital transformation, and cybersecurity in large-scale operations.

IBPAP Industry Briefing: Ms. Zoe Diaz De Rivera provided eye-opening statistics - the IT-BPM sector employs 1.7 million Filipinos and drives innovation through automation, AI, and RPA. Key takeaway: technical skills get you noticed, but communication, critical thinking, and adaptability get you hired.

Afternoon at Google Philippines BGC with Atty. Yves Gonzalez (Head of Government Affairs & Public Policy) was transformative. Learned about Google's AI evolution, digital inclusion initiatives like "Be Internet Awesome," and the critical importance of ethical tech governance. The office tour showcased innovation-driven workplace design that sparked our imagination about future tech careers.

Day 3 - June 10 ‚Ä¢ Skills Development & AI Innovation

Morning at Teleperformance Philippines (SM Aura BGC) with Ms. Beatrice Grace Bonjibod revealed TP's evolution beyond traditional customer support into digitally integrated business services. Key insight: 53% of their workforce are women, showcasing their commitment to gender equality. A peaceful lunch break at the Manila American Cemetery and Memorial in BGC provided quiet reflection time surrounded by beautifully maintained grounds. Ralph Vincent Regalado, Chief Scientist and Head of AI, welcomed us with insights into collaborative technology solutions. Jazmine Calma taught us "Why Culture Matters" for product excellence. Toni-Jan Keith Monserrat's "Applied AI: From Classroom to Real Use Cases" connected our theoretical knowledge to real-world applications in agriculture, customer service, and health. Miguel Siriban's portfolio workshop gave actionable tips on showcasing projects and storytelling through design.

Day 6 - June 13 ‚Ä¢ Baguio City Exploration

1 AM departure for the City of Pines! Early morning visit to Benguet Strawberry Farm - where we truly felt the cold mountain air of Baguio embracing us. Fresh strawberries and crisp mountain atmosphere were the perfect introduction to highland life.

Our peaceful visit to the serene Chinese Bell Church provided quiet moments for spiritual reflection amidst our busy itinerary. The traditional architecture and tranquil atmosphere offered a perfect contrast to the bustling tourist spots.

Next, we toured the prestigious Philippine Military Academy (PMA) where we witnessed the impressive sight of trainee cadets marching in perfect formation - a display of discipline and excellence that left us in awe of military precision.

We then explored The Mansion with its beautiful colonial architecture and well-maintained gardens, appreciating the historical significance and elegant design of this government residence.

At Mines View Park, we captured countless photos with the breathtaking mountain vistas as our backdrop. The panoramic views of the mining town below were spectacular, but the highlight was making wishes at the famous wishing well - each of us dropping coins while silently hoping our dreams for our CS careers would come true.

The day's unforgettable finale: Burnham Park boat riding - navigating the lake with friends brought endless laughter and a few near-collisions that made it even more memorable! The cold Baguio air, combined with friendship and adventure, created perfect memories.
